About Us

Establish in 2007, Apostle Paul Memorial Park or Aringay, La Union, Inc. (APMP), continues its tradition of services, care, and goodwill ot the Filipino people. APMP is a 29-hectare memorial park development in the heart of La Union, which lush landscapes and well-maintained park grounds, equipped with utitlities such as electrical, drainage, irrigation, and security.
