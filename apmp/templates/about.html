About Us

Establish in 2007, Apostle Paul Memorial Park of Aringay, La Union, Inc. (APMP), continues its tradition of services, care, and goodwill ot the Filipino people. APMP is a 29-hectare memorial park development in the heart of La Union, which offers lush landscapes and well-maintained park grounds, equipped with utilities such as electrical, drainage, irrigation, and security.

The development of the APMP is hinged on tranquility and serenity. The wide open spaces and beautiful array of trees blend beautifully with the layout of the memorial park, which ensures a peaceful and calm atmosphere. With its motto - "Ushering you to the next life", APMP is committed to providing world-class service for a reasonable price, as epitomized by its team of professionals and well-trained staff who exemplify the values of dignity, compassion, respect, caring service, and attention to detail.

APMP not only focuses on our dearly departed but also on the bereaved families. For this reason, APMP is conveniently located in a secure setting with environmentally-sound structures, which makes it a great place for meaningful reflection and fond remembrance of your loved ones.
